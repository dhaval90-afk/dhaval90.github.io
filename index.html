Who I Am
About Me

Hi, I’m Dhaval - a B2B sales leader, customer success professional, and former founder focused on building growth through trust, clarity, and long-term value. Over the past 8+ years I've worn many hats — individual contributor, team lead and player-coach — with a strong focus on B2B SaaS sales and select B2C experience across India and North American markets. That breadth hasn't just given me range; it's given me perspective on what truly drives a buying decision regardless of the industry or geography.

What I'm most proud of isn't a single deal or a quota number — it's the relationships I've built along the way. Clients who started as cold prospects have become long-term partners, and that's no accident. I invest in understanding people deeply, earning trust slowly, and delivering on every promise I make.

Beyond the numbers, I'm known for being a genuine listener — someone who takes time to understand a client's real challenges before proposing a solution. I believe great sales is really great storytelling, and I bring that into every pitch, presentation and conversation. I'm also passionate about lifting others up, whether that's mentoring a junior rep or sharing what I've learned along the way.
